About

Dr A.P.J Abdul Kalam Technical University (AKTU), formerly Uttar Pradesh Technical University (UPTU), is a public collegiate university in Lucknow, Uttar Pradesh, India. AKTU offers undergraduate and postgraduate courses in engineering, technology, management, and many other fields of study.
